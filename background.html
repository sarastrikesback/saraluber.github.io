Background

Rooted in curiosity, care, and community

My path to environmental communications has always been shaped by people. I grew up watching how access to clean air, reliable data, and clear information can make or break a community’s ability to thrive. That perspective grounds every project I take on.

I live in Raleigh, North Carolina, and I’m open to relocating so I can keep supporting environmentally focused teams wherever they need me. Outside of work, you’ll find me sewing, baking, reading, and creating experiences that invite people in.

Beyond the desk

Personal interests that keep me grounded

Creative practices and community care rituals fuel the energy I bring to environmental work.

Sewing & textile design

Pattern drafting and textile sourcing remind me how supply chains touch every community differently.

Baking for connection

Sharing bread, pies, and cookies is my favorite way to bring people together and fuel difficult conversations with generosity.

Reading & research

Whether it’s policy analysis or speculative fiction, reading widens my understanding of whose voices are centered—and whose are missing.
