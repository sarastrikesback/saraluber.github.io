Assistant to the Office Director

U.S. Environmental Protection Agency
Office of Research and Development — Office of Resource Management

Served as a key advisor to the Office Director during a major administrative transition and reduction in federal staff, taking on responsibilities typically held by the Senior Advisor, Communications Lead, Chief of Staff, and Program Operations Director. Delivered high-impact briefings, memos, and presentations to large audiences, translating complex environmental and operational data into clear, actionable insights. Created data-driven dashboards and visual materials using Microsoft 365 tools to guide executive decision-making and resource allocation. Developed and implemented Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to improve coordination and workflow efficiency across ORD divisions. Oversaw executive scheduling, correspondence, and priority tracking, contributing to strategic and operational decisions while maintaining discretion with pre-decisional materials.

Notable Skills: Strategic Communication, Executive Decision Support, Microsoft 365, Data Visualization, Federal Policy Alignment.

Program Operations Administrator

U.S. Environmental Protection Agency

Energy Policy Research Analyst
