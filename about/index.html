About Sara

Systems-minded storytelling grounded in community care

I’m an environmental communicator based in Raleigh, North Carolina. My work sits at the intersection of policy, data, and care—helping teams surface human stories inside complex environmental programs.

Collaborations with federal agencies, clean energy coalitions, and local partners taught me how to translate technical insights into accessible narratives. I love designing processes that help teams stay aligned while we honor the communities most impacted by climate change.

How I show up to every collaboration

  • Embedded partner Co-design workflows, document SOPs, and share progress so cross-functional teams can trust the process.
  • Story-first data Translate research and analytics into clear, empathetic messages that reach executive and community audiences.
  • Care for place Keep environmental justice and local knowledge at the center of every communication plan.

Values that guide my work

Community care

I grew up in Maryland and now call North Carolina home. Daily walks through Raleigh’s parks and coastal trips to the Outer Banks keep me grounded in why environmental storytelling matters.

Transparency

From executive briefings to community toolkits, I prioritize clarity, context, and documentation that makes the next step obvious for everyone involved.

Curiosity

My background in data analysis fuels thoughtful questions, rigorous research, and communications that feel both human and actionable.
