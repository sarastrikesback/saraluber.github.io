About Sara

Systems-minded storytelling grounded in community

I’m an environmental communicator and operations partner based in Raleigh, North Carolina. My work sits at the intersection of policy, data, and care—helping teams surface the human stories inside complex environmental programs.

I’m energized by collaborations that let me embed with mission-driven teams and translate technical insights into plain language for the communities they serve.
