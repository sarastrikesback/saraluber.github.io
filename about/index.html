About Sara

Systems-minded storytelling grounded in community care

I’m an environmental communicator based in Raleigh, North Carolina. My work sits at the intersection of policy, data, and care—helping teams surface human stories inside complex environmental programs.

Collaborations with federal agencies, clean energy coalitions, and local partners taught me how to translate technical insights into accessible narratives. I am enthusiastic about designing processes that help teams stay aligned while we honor the communities most impacted by climate change.
