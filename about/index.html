About Sara

Systems-minded storytelling grounded in community care

I’m an environmental communicator based in Raleigh, North Carolina. My work sits at the intersection of policy, data, and care—helping teams surface human stories inside complex environmental programs.

Collaborations with federal agencies, clean energy coalitions, and local partners taught me how to translate technical insights into accessible narratives. I enjoy building processes that keep teams aligned while honoring the communities most impacted by climate change.

How I work

Clear, human-centered communication for complex systems

I help teams connect research and policy to the people those decisions affect—using plain language, good visuals, and reliable operations.

Translate complexity

Turn technical findings into narratives and materials that are accurate, accessible, and action-oriented.

Build alignment

Create systems, SOPs, and lightweight planning tools so teams stay coordinated across stakeholders.

Center equity

Design communications that respect time, reduce barriers, and amplify community expertise.
