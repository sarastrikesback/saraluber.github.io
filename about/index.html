About Sara

Systems-minded storytelling grounded in community care

I’m an environmental communicator and operations partner based in Raleigh, North Carolina. My work sits at the intersection of policy, data, and care—helping teams surface human stories inside complex environmental programs.

Collaborations with public agencies, clean energy coalitions, and community partners taught me how to translate technical insights into plain language and facilitate equitable decisions.

Embedded with teams

I thrive when I’m part of the crew—co-designing workflows, documenting SOPs, and keeping moving pieces aligned.

Story-first data

Dashboards, memos, and briefings should resonate. I translate research into narratives stakeholders can use.

Care for place

From neighborhood parks to coastal ecosystems, I carry a deep commitment to the communities most impacted by climate change.
